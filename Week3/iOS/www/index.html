Community Involvement

StreetLinks is proud to be a leader in the mortgage industry and in the communities we represent! Throughout the year, StreetLinks is involved in many community events and we also encourage employee involvement in local and national charitable causes.

StreetLinks Gives Back

Blood Drives
We partner with the  Indiana Blood Center  for at least one employee blood drive per year.

Food Drives
StreetLinks holds regular team and company-wide contests to see who can raise the most food for local pantries. 

The StreetLinks Green Team
Our Green Team has helped us abandon bad habits and reduce our carbon footprint company-wide through promoting small tasks like turning off the bathroom lights and starting office recycling programs.

Employee Causes
Often, employees will reach out to the company for support for causes they believe in. We do our best to communicate those causes within the company so that all employees have the opportunity to get involved.

The Annual Backyard Bash Charity Event

StreetLinks' Annual Backyard Bash is held each summer at our headquarters in Indianapolis and we will be expanding to our additional facilities for future events. Each year, we reach out to local businesses and partners for prize donations which are raffled off to StreetLinks employees and their families at the event. All proceeds are given to the local and national charitable foundations chosen by the StreetLinks team. At 2011's 3rd Annual Backyard Bash, we raised $10,500 and donated 3,281 pounds of canned and nonperishable food items to a local pantry.

Get Involved!

Interested in donating to 2013's 5th Annual Backyard Bash? Contact StreetLinks Community Development Team today at  community@streetlinks.com

StreetLinks Leadership

Steve Haslam  -  CEO

Steve is a 20 year mortgage industry veteran who has led all aspects of national retail and wholesale platforms. Steve also led one of the nation's largest captive appraisal management companies. Steve joined StreetLinks because, "as a former customer ordering thousands of appraisals per month, StreetLinks was the only company who consistently met the demands of everyone in the lending cycle - from LO to Processor to Underwriter to Investor; given the current lending and regulatory environment, and having been a customer of almost every major traditional appraisal management company in my career, StreetLinks is positioned as the 'best of breed' to meet the appraisal needs of this market."

Tom Hurst  -  President

As an original founder of StreetLinks, Tom has played a pivotal role in the company’s growth and success since leaving the aviation industry in 2002. Tom’s passion and unwavering dedication to quality, service and innovation are contagious, and have helped to propel both StreetLinks and the mortgage industry forward for over 10 years. Tom’s transition from SVP to EVP to President at StreetLinks has allowed him to expand his focus and become an even more integral part of StreetLinks’ growth, product development and commitment to pushing the status quo.

Chad Barker  -  Chief Operating Officer

Chad joined Superior Appraisal (the forerunner of StreetLinks) in 1996 upon graduation from Indiana University. As a licensed appraiser and astute business developer, Chad was instrumental in the rapid expansion of Superior's client base while simultaneously building Superior's largest team of in-house appraisers. As the company diversified into a national appraisal management company, Chad helped to pioneer the best-of-breed processing and operations platform that differentiates StreetLinks from it's competition today. With a strong focus on business culture, exceptional customer service, and technological systems, Chad continues to execute the ongoing management needs of StreetLinks' unique appraisal quality and delivery platform.

Matthew Lautz ,  Chief Information Officer

As Chief Information Officer, Matthew Lautz is responsible for overseeing all software product development and infrastructure. With a ten year career in building and managing software companies, Lautz started his first software development company at the age of 16. Since, Lautz has held executive leadership roles in several technology and telecom related companies, most recently as President of Corvisa LLC, a lending technology firm which was acquired by StreetLinks in 2010.

Kelly Taylor  -  SVP, National Sales

Kelly has 20 years experience in the mortgage industry managing national retail branch systems, multiple location call centers, as well as large broker platforms. With a strong reputation in the industry for outstanding results in both sales and quality he was a perfect fit to assume national sales responsibility for StreetLinks. Having been a mortgage industry leading sales leader he brings a unique perspective to the Appraisal Management business as well as having a strong understanding of the issues facing today's mortgage sales leaders.

Donna DelMonte  -  SVP, Product Development

Donna has over 20 years in the mortgage industry managing National Wholesale and Retail lending operations, encompassing the loan process from origination, underwriting, secondary and risk management. Having formally held the position of Chief Credit Officer, Donna brings a wealth of experience in understanding the necessary service expectations and tools needed for StreetLinks clients. The level of knowledge and experience Donna brings to StreetLinks allows her to understand the needs of the clients to evaluate and create products to better enhance efficiency, and create better, reliable products to mitigate risk potential for our customers.

Jaime Brownfield  -  Vice President, Processing

Jaime began her career with StreetLinks as a frontline employee early in 2007, and has since grown rapidly with the company. With more than 18 years of customer service experience, she understands how each part of the operation functions. She has been instrumental in building relationships both externally and internally, defining processes and procedures and creating techniques to improve the organization in both productivity and quality. Along with her customer service background and knowledge of the operation; Jaime’s abilities to teach, coach, train and mentor are at the heart of her continued growth with Streetlinks and the company’s success.

StreetLinks History

A Look Back at StreetLinks Lender Solutions ®

Our roots date back to 1995 when current StreetLinks Chief Strategy Officer, Tony Ebeyer, founded Superior Appraisal Services, which grew to be the largest and most technologically advanced appraisal firm in the Midwest. Four former Superior appraisers still hold key management positions at StreetLinks today.

In 2008, Superior's innovation and focus on quality, timely appraisals was recognized as industry-leading in light of the new HVCC regulations. The company was acquired in part by NovaStar Financial, bringing with it key management staff, including current CEO, Steve Haslam. The company was renamed StreetLinks National Appraisal Services and quickly became one of the fastest growing, most reputable appraisal management companies (AMCs) in the industry.

Bringing Change to the Mortgage Valuation Industry

Today under the StreetLinks Lender Solutions brand we continue to expand and enhance our valuation offerings to maintain the highest level of quality and service available on the market and serve as a true solution partner to our clients.
